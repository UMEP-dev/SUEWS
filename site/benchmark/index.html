Regression benchmark

The benchmark suite

SUEWS releases scored against eddy-covariance observations at urban flux sites.

Each site scores the modelled energy balance against tower observations. One site is benchmarked so far — London, on v2026.4.3 — establishing a baseline; later releases are scored against it to flag regressions. Further sites are planned.
