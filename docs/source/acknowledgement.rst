
Acknowledgements
================

SUEWS is the work of an international team, funded by many grants, and
built on a number of open-source libraries. The full credits are
maintained on the project site at `suews.io <https://suews.io/>`_ so
they stay in sync with each release and with the public-facing pages.

Team and contributors
---------------------

The names of everyone who has shipped physics, tooling, or documentation
into SUEWS — past and present — are listed at
`suews.io/team <https://suews.io/team/>`_.

Funding
-------

Grants and institutional support that funded SUEWS model development and
supportive observations are listed at
`suews.io/funding <https://suews.io/funding/>`_.

Dependencies
------------

The open-source libraries that SUEWS builds on are listed at
`suews.io/dependencies <https://suews.io/dependencies/>`_.

.. note::

   If you spot an omission or an error on any of these pages, please
   open an issue on `GitHub <https://github.com/UMEP-dev/SUEWS/issues>`_
   and we will correct it.
