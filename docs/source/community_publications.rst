Community Publications
======================

Publications and projects using SUEWS from the community. Topic
filtering mirrors :ref:`Recent_publications` — every entry carries at
least one topic slug from the controlled vocabulary documented in the
header of ``assets/refs/refs-community.bib``, and the same
``#pub-<slug>`` anchors are available here once a topic has
contributions.

.. note::
	**Add your SUEWS publication**

	`Submit a PR to add your BibTeX entry <https://github.com/UMEP-dev/SUEWS/edit/master/docs/source/assets/refs/refs-community.bib>`_.

	Please include a comment above your entry describing how SUEWS was
	used, and tag it with at least one slug from the controlled
	vocabulary:

	- ``energy-balance`` — surface energy balance, flux partitioning
	- ``water-balance`` — urban hydrology, evapotranspiration, snow, irrigation
	- ``storage-heat`` — delta-QS parameterisation (OHM lineage)
	- ``radiation`` — NARP, SOLWEIG, mean radiant temperature
	- ``anthropogenic-heat`` — QF modelling, urban heat emissions
	- ``carbon-flux`` — urban CO\ :sub:`2` exchange, tree sequestration
	- ``building-energy`` — urban meteorology for building energy simulations
	- ``model-infrastructure`` — model code, coupling, forcing workflows

	Example: ``keywords = {water-balance, vegetation-phenology}``.

	We welcome all SUEWS-related work: peer-reviewed papers, conference
	proceedings, theses, technical reports, and educational materials.
