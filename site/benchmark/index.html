The benchmark suite

Every SUEWS release is re-run against eddy-covariance observations in cities worldwide — a standing scoreboard of model fidelity.

Each site scores the modelled energy balance against tower measurements; a release is flagged the moment any flux degrades against its reference. Three of four sites clear the current release, v2026.4.3.
