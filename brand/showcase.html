Design Philosophy

The SUEWS logo distils the model's core function: simulating energy and water exchanges in urban environments.

  • Sun — Solar radiation driving the surface energy balance; the warm gradient represents shortwave and longwave radiation fluxes
  • Tree — Urban vegetation (parks, street trees, gardens) that SUEWS models as a key land cover type driving evapotranspiration and shading
  • Buildings — Urban morphology with varying heights representing the heterogeneous built environment that SUEWS characterises
  • Water wave — The hydrological cycle: precipitation, runoff, evaporation, and storage that form the water balance component
  • Circle — The bounded urban domain, a contained system where all fluxes are accounted for

The composition follows golden ratio proportions, with the sun positioned at the upper-right intersection point — where energy enters the system.
