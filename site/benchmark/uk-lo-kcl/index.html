Regression benchmark / UK-LO-KCL

Energy balance at King’s College London

A standing check: does each SUEWS release still reproduce the observed urban energy balance as well as the last?

Evaluated against eddy-covariance flux observations at the Kc1 tower, following Ward et al. (2016). Each release is scored on the bias (MBE) and absolute error (MAE) of its modelled fluxes; a release is flagged when any flux degrades against the reference.

▲ Regression flagged
