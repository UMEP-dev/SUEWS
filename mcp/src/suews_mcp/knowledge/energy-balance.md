# Urban Energy Balance

SUEWS represents the surface energy balance as:

Q* + QF = QH + QE + QS

where:
- `Q*` is net all-wave radiation;
- `QF` is anthropogenic heat flux;
- `QH` is sensible heat flux;
- `QE` is latent heat flux;
- `QS` is storage heat flux.

Interpretation guidance:
- Large daytime `QH` often indicates strong heating of near-surface air.
- Larger `QE` usually reflects stronger evaporation/transpiration.
- `QS` captures short-term storage and release in urban materials and can delay evening cooling.

When reviewing results, check both means and timing.
A balanced mean can still hide unrealistic diurnal dynamics.
