The Team

Built by a community, since 2011

SUEWS has been built over more than a decade by researchers at the University of Reading, University College London, University of Helsinki, University of Gothenburg, and collaborators worldwide.

Contributors are grouped below by scope of involvement — active leads, major contributors past and present, and further contributors who shipped specific physics, tooling, or documentation. For the research grants that funded this work and the open-source libraries the core builds on, see Funding and Dependencies.
