Design Philosophy

The logo embraces natural, flowing forms that mirror the continuous exchange of energy and water in urban environments. These traced contours reflect how real-world processes blend and interact.

  • Sun Gold — Outer disk representing incoming solar radiation; the primary energy input that drives all urban climate processes
  • Energy Orange — Inner core symbolising thermal energy; sensible and latent heat fluxes that SUEWS models at the urban surface
  • Sky Blue — Urban skyline silhouette; buildings against the atmosphere where surface-air energy exchanges occur
  • Vegetation Green — Organic leaf sweeping across the composition; evapotranspiration, urban greenery, and living systems
  • Wave Blue — Deep blue flowing curve at the base; the water balance foundation — precipitation, runoff, storage, evaporation
  • Urban Slate — Used for icon disk backgrounds and text; a sophisticated neutral that grounds the vibrant palette

The style preserves natural irregularities that echo how energy and water actually flow through cities — never in perfect lines, always in dynamic equilibrium.

Design Approach

As researchers and developers rather than design professionals, we used AI tools to assist with branding. The process involved:

  1. Prototyping with Gemini 3 Pro Image (Nano Banana Pro) — Initial concepts generated from prompts describing SUEWS's core scientific elements: energy balance, hydrological cycle, urban form and natural landscape.
  2. Iterative refinement — Designs simplified for clear communication at various sizes
  3. Vector conversion with Claude Opus 4.5 — Raster images traced and converted to clean SVG format for scalability
