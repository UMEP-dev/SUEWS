Regression benchmark / UK-LO-KCL

Energy balance at King’s College London

Modelled energy-balance fluxes at the Kc1 tower, scored against eddy-covariance observations.

Reference observations: Ward et al. (2016). Each flux is scored by mean bias (MBE) and mean absolute error (MAE) against the observations. This is the first SUEWS release on record for this site; later releases are scored against it to flag regressions.

Release

Only v2026.4.3 has been run so far; earlier releases will be backfilled to build the regression history.

Baseline established

v2026.4.3 scored against the Ward et al. (2016) observations. Mean absolute error spans 3.0–37.8 W m−2 across the five fluxes; sensible heat QH, the energy-balance residual, carries the largest error.
